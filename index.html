Welcome to my website!

I'm a Ph.D. candidate at the University College Dublin (UCD) under the supervision of Karl Whelan and co-supervised by Constantin Burgi.

My research focuses on Inflation Expectations and Monetary Policy using different expectations survey data. In particular, my current projects investigate the relationship between Inflation and Oil forecasts using Survey of Professional forecasters and Consumers Expectations data.

I have a BA in Economics from University of Delhi , and a Master's degree in Economics from PSE and Ecole des Hautes Études en Sciences Sociales (EHESS) and Financial Economics from Madras School of Economics (MSE).
